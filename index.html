Some common questions
were often asked


How do you choose a plant?

Consider your local climate and sunlight conditions. Choose a plant that suits the available space and meets your care preferences.

How do I change the pot?

Select a larger pot with drainage holes. Gently remove the plant from its current pot, place it in the new one, and fill with fresh soil, ensuring the roots are well-covered.

Why are gnats flying around my plant?

Overwatering may create a damp environment attracting gnats. Allow the soil to dry between watering to discourage their presence.

What causes leaves to become pale?

Pale leaves can indicate nutrient deficiencies, often due to poor soil nutrition or improper pH levels.

What causes brown, crispy leaves?

Brown, crispy leaves may result from underwatering or low humidity, leading to moisture loss. Ensure consistent watering and consider increasing humidity around the plant.

My flowers are falling off or dying?

Flower drop can occur due to stress factors like inadequate sunlight, overwatering, or nutrient imbalances. Assess and address these factors to promote flower health and retention.
